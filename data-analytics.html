Vendor Performance Analytics

View on GitHub

An end-to-end vendor analytics pipeline using SQL, Python, Power BI (DAX), and business reporting to convert raw transactions into actionable procurement insights.

  • Top vendor concentration: Top 10 vendors contribute 65.69% of purchases, highlighting supply chain dependency risk.
  • Bulk purchase savings: Bulk buying reduces unit costs by ~72% ($10.78 vs. $39.05 for small orders).
  • Inventory turnover: Identified $2.71M tied up in unsold inventory and flagged low-turnover vendors.
  • Statistical validation: t-test confirms profit margin differences between top vs. low vendors are statistically significant.

Trading Strategies vs. Mutual Fund Analysis

View on GitHub

A quantitative analysis comparing nine technical-indicator trading strategies against the TD Bank NASDAQ Index Mutual Fund (TDB981) using historical backtesting.

  • Implemented 9 strategies (EMA, Stochastic Oscillator, MACD, Trailing Stop, Volatility Breakout, Timeframe Filtering, Mean Reversion, Heikin Ashi, VWMA).
  • Backtested 2014–2023 data and evaluated win rate, average return, Sharpe ratio, cumulative return, and risk.
  • Result highlight: EMA achieved the highest average return (23.42%) in the study.
